dmca

at kiwi, we are committed to safeguarding intellectual property and fully comply with the digital millennium copyright act (dmca). we strive to ensure that all content on our platform, including games, adheres to copyright regulations.

1. submitting a dmca notice

if you believe your copyrighted work has been used on our site without authorization, please submit a detailed notice to our dmca agent at: kiwi.tk@mail.com

your notice should include:

  • your electronic or physical signature, confirming that you own the copyright or are authorized to act on behalf of the owner.
  • a clear description of the copyrighted material you believe has been infringed.
  • specific details about the allegedly infringing content on our site, including a direct link.
  • your contact details, including your full name, email address, mailing address, and phone number.
  • a statement made in good faith asserting that the disputed use is not authorized by the copyright owner, their representative, or the law.
  • a declaration, under penalty of perjury, confirming the accuracy of your notice and your authority to act on behalf of the copyright owner.

2. our response

upon receiving your notice, we will promptly investigate and take necessary action, which may include removing or restricting access to the disputed content. the user responsible for the material will also be informed and given a chance to submit a counter-notice if they believe the removal was a mistake.

3. important notice

please note that submitting a false dmca claim may have legal consequences. if you are uncertain about whether the content in question violates your copyright, we recommend consulting legal counsel before filing a complaint.

4. commitment to intellectual property

we appreciate your cooperation in protecting intellectual property on kiwi.
